Message for Our Teacher

To our very cutesy Ma'am Roan,

As we reach the end of our final term, we want to take a moment to express our heartfelt gratitude for your guidance, support, and unwavering dedication throughout our web development journey.

You’ve not only taught us the technical skills we needed but also inspired us to think creatively and challenge ourselves to build something extraordinary. Your patience and enthusiasm made every lesson engaging, and your encouragement pushed us to grow both as learners and as individuals.

One thing we’ll always remember about you is how you try so hard to act strict—“laging tinatry maging masungit kahit hindi naman talaga siya masungit”—but we all know deep down that you have a kind heart. “Hindi siya marunong maging masama; she's bad at pretending to be a bad person.” These little quirks made you even more endearing and memorable to us.

We will carry forward the knowledge, skills, and values you’ve instilled in us, and we hope to make you proud as we continue on our paths.

Thank you for everything, Ma’am Roan. You will always hold a special place in our hearts. This isn’t a goodbye, but rather a “see you again,” as your influence will remain with us in all our future endeavors.

With much respect and gratitude,
Lyka
